How We Got There

Four moves that made the difference.

01

Stayed visible before demand peaked

Office project buyers do not convert instantly. Consistent visibility helped BAFCO stay familiar before the buying window opened, so January was a harvest, not a cold start.

02

Shifted from products to outcomes

Workspace-focused creative made the end result easier to imagine than product-only messaging. Buyers saw themselves in the space before they clicked.

03

Matched search intent more tightly

Search traffic was aligned more closely to what users were actively looking for, with more relevant landing destinations and less friction between intent and action.

04

Let performance move the budget

Budget allocation became more dynamic, reducing waste and concentrating spend into what was working. Efficiency compounded across the month.

January did not create the result.
It exposed it.

What looked like a peak month win was the outcome of a year of stronger visibility, better creative direction, tighter intent alignment, and more disciplined optimisation. The season opened the door. The system walked through it.
