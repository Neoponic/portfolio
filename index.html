How I think

Most marketing roles are too narrow. I'm building toward the opposite. A generalist who can see the whole machine, fix the broken parts, and ship the system that runs after the meeting ends. Strategy without execution is theatre. Execution without strategy is busy work. The interesting work happens when the same person owns both.

So, what now?

If something on this site made you nod, write to me. Roles, projects, or just to compare notes, all welcome.
